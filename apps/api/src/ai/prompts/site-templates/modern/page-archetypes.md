## Gym Website Page Archetypes for AI Content Generation

### 1. Home Page (`/`)

*   **Funnel stage**: **Awareness** (just discovered us, looking for a gym)
*   **Visitor's question at this stage**: "What kind of gym is this, and can it help *me* achieve my fitness goals?" / "Is this gym for people like me?"
*   **What the page must deliver**:
    *   Immediate, clear communication of the gym's core offering (e.g., CrossFit, Weightlifting, Yoga, HIIT) and target audience.
    *   A compelling transformation promise (e.g., "Get stronger," "Improve endurance," "Lose weight," "Find a community").
    *   A sense of the gym's unique atmosphere or culture.
    *   Clear pathways to explore specific programs or take a low-commitment first step.
*   **Proof and evidence for this stage**:
    *   High-quality visuals (photos/videos) showing diverse members actively working out and interacting.
    *   Short, impactful testimonials (quotes with photos) highlighting key benefits or community aspects.
    *   Optional: "As seen in" logos if applicable, or small trust badges (e.g., "Certified Coaches").
*   **Primary conversion action**: `business.primaryCta` (e.g., "Book a Free Intro Class," "Try Your First Class Free," "Start Your Fitness Journey").
*   **SEO requirements for this page**:
    *   **H1**: Outcome-focused and location-aware (e.g., "Achieve Your Fitness Goals in [City Name]," "Build Strength & Community in [City Name]," "The Best CrossFit Gym in [City Name]").
    *   Body content should naturally integrate keywords like "gym [city]", "fitness [city]", "workout [city]".
    *   No specific word count minimum, but rich, descriptive content is encouraged for engagement and SEO.

### 2. Program Pages (e.g., `/crossfit`, `/yoga`, `/personal-training`)

*   **Funnel stage**: **Consideration** (comparing options, understanding specific offerings)
*   **Visitor's question at this stage**: "What exactly is this program, who is it for, and what results can I expect?" / "Will this program work for me and my fitness level?"
*   **What the page must deliver**:
    *   Detailed explanation of the program (what it is, philosophy, benefits).
    *   Clear identification of the target audience (beginners, experienced athletes, specific goals).
    *   Description of a typical session or class structure.
    *   Expected outcomes and transformations for members.
    *   Answers to common questions about the program.
*   **Proof and evidence for this stage**:
    *   Before/after photos (if appropriate and consent is given).
    *   Member testimonials specifically about the program's effectiveness or their experience.
    *   Coach credentials relevant to the program (e.g., "Certified CrossFit Level 2 Coach").
    *   Short video clips of the program in action.
    *   Statistics on program success or member retention (e.g., "90% of members improve their PRs").
