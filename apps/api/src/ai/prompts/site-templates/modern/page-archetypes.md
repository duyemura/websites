## Gym Website Page Archetypes for AI Content Generation

### General Principles for AI Content Generation:

*   **Tone:** Enthusiastic, encouraging, empowering, professional, and authentic.
*   **Keywords:** Incorporate location-specific keywords (e.g., "[City] gym", "[City] fitness"), program-specific keywords (e.g., "CrossFit [City]", "yoga classes [City]"), and benefit-oriented keywords (e.g., "weight loss [City]", "strength training [City]").
*   **Readability:** Use clear, concise language. Employ headings, bullet points, and short paragraphs to improve scannability.
*   **Call to Action (CTA) Integration:** CTAs should be strategically placed and clearly visible on every page, guiding the user to the next logical step.

### Page Archetype: Home Page (`/`)

*   **Funnel Stage**: Awareness / Consideration (initial discovery, gathering first impressions)
*   **Visitor's Question at this Stage**: "What kind of gym is this, and is it for someone like me?" or "Can this gym help me achieve my fitness goals?"
*   **What the Page Must Deliver**:
    *   Immediate communication of the gym's core identity (e.g., "Strength & Conditioning," "Yoga & Wellness," "Boxing & HIIT").
    *   A clear transformation promise (e.g., "Get Stronger, Healthier, and More Confident").
    *   A strong sense of community and support.
    *   Easy access to key offerings and next steps.
*   **Proof and Evidence for this Stage**:
    *   High-quality imagery/video showcasing diverse, happy members in action.
    *   Short, impactful testimonials highlighting member transformations or positive experiences (TestimonialBand).
    *   Visual representation of location/community (LocationBlock).
*   **Primary Conversion Action**: `business.primaryCta` (e.g., "Book a Free Intro Class," "Try Your First Class Free").
*   **SEO Requirements for this Page**:
