This template best suits a **Boutique Fitness Studio** or a **Personal Training Studio**. The `HeroCenter` component with its clean background `rgb(241, 241, 241)` and the `FeatureGridEven` with similar light aesthetics suggest a polished, modern feel rather than the raw energy of CrossFit or the traditional gym's utilitarianism. The `ProgramCardsSticky` implies a curated offering, typical of specialized studios. The lack of aggressive typography or dark, high-contrast elements further supports a more refined, less intense environment.

**Brand voice register**: Premium/exclusive. The copy should convey a sense of elevated experience and personalized attention, focusing on quality and tailored results.

**Hero copy guidance**: The headline should evoke a sense of **transformation** and **empowerment**. The subheadline should clarify the studio's unique offering and its local connection, e.g., "Elevate your fitness journey at [City]'s premier personal training studio."

**Testimonial style**: Narrative and detailed, focusing on the *journey* and *overall experience* rather than just numerical results. They should highlight the personalized approach and the studio's atmosphere, e.g., "I've never felt so supported in my fitness goals since joining [Gym Name] in [City]."

**Program description style**: Lead with the **outcome** and the **transformation** clients can expect. The language should be aspirational and accessible, avoiding overly technical jargon, but still convey the expertise behind the program.

**CTA tone**: Always low-commitment. The primary CTA should be `business.primaryCta` (e.g., "Book a free consultation"). The header must carry a persistent CTA button. Every section should offer a path to `business.primaryCta`.

**Imagery direction**: Clean lifestyle photography featuring diverse individuals engaged in focused, mindful exercise. Emphasize personalized attention and the studio's aesthetic. Avoid gritty action shots or overly posed, generic stock photos. Focus on the feeling of accomplishment and well-being.
