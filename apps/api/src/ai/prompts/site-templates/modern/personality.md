This template best suits a **Boutique Fitness Studio** or a **Personal Training Studio**. The typography, with its clean sans-serifs and ample white space, combined with the "HeroCenter" component suggesting a prominent, singular message, points to a more curated and exclusive experience rather than the raw energy of CrossFit or the vastness of a traditional gym. The "FeatureGridEven" and "ProgramCardsSticky" components imply a focus on specific, well-defined offerings.

**Brand Voice Register**: Premium/exclusive. The AI copywriter should craft sophisticated and aspirational language that highlights quality, personalized attention, and a transformative experience.

**Hero Copy Guidance**: The headline should evoke **transformation** and **empowerment**. The subheadline should clarify the unique benefit or experience offered, perhaps emphasizing personalized guidance or exceptional results. For example: "Unlock Your Potential at [City]'s Premier Fitness Studio." The subheadline could then expand on the bespoke experience.

**Testimonial Style**: Testimonials should be narrative and detailed, focusing on the *journey* and the *impact* on the individual's life, rather than just raw numbers. They should highlight the atmosphere, the expertise of the trainers, and the feeling of achievement.

**Program Description Style**: Lead with the **outcome** and the **personalized experience**. The language should be polished and articulate, but not overly technical, focusing on the benefits and the "why" behind the method.

**CTA Tone**: Always low-commitment. The primary CTA everywhere should be `business.primaryCta`. The header must carry a persistent CTA button. Every section should offer a path to that same low-commitment action.

**Imagery Direction**: Clean lifestyle photography showcasing individuals in focused, elegant motion, or polished studio aesthetics with a minimalist feel. Avoid gritty action shots or overly crowded community celebration photos. Focus on individual empowerment and the serene, high-quality environment.
